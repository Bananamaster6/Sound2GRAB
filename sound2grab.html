What this tool can and can't do: GRAB's sound block is a single-oscillator retro synth — one waveform, one pitch, one envelope (same model as sfxr/bfxr). It's built for short blips, lasers, jumps, and coin-style effects. It cannot reproduce music, voice, or layered/textured audio — no amount of analysis makes one oscillator sound like a full recording. For best results, feed it short, punchy, mostly single-toned sounds, and trim away any silence or noise at the edges first.
